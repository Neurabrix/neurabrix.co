Doctor Dashboard

The Doctor Dashboard gives clinicians one place to review the live queue, understand patient context, and begin the right clinical workflow without switching between separate screens.

Live queue Booked and walk-in patients stay visible in the first screen.
Patient context Registration, appointment timing, queue number, and diagnosis readiness are shown together.
First action The doctor can move from queue review into notes or patient detail.
Product walkthrough: live queue review, patient context, and first clinical action. Captions describe each step. Open video

Workflow shown

#
1

Open the doctor dashboard

The portal opens with the clinician identity and today queue already loaded.

2

Review the queue table

The doctor sees position, patient name, registration number, visit time, queue number, and diagnosis state in one compact table.

3

Confirm the active patient context

The first queued patient remains clear so the clinician can start from the correct record.

4

Move into patient work

The dashboard exposes the path into notes and patient detail without making the doctor search again.

What this helps with

#

Faster queue review

Clinicians can scan today’s roster and find the next patient without opening a separate scheduling view.

Better visit continuity

Queue details and patient context stay together so handoff from front desk to doctor is easier to follow.

Lower navigation overhead

The dashboard is a single starting point for notes, patient detail, and visit-day work.
